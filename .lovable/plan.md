
# Personal Portfolio — College Journey

A clean, minimal-dark multi-page portfolio documenting your years at college, with three featured highlights and a full activity log.

## Structure & Navigation
- **Top navigation bar** (sticky): Home · About · Highlights (dropdown to the 3) · Activities · with subtle hover underlines
- **Pages**:
  - `/` — Home
  - `/about` — About me
  - `/highlights/:slug` — One page per highlight (3 total)
  - `/activities` — Full activity list
- Footer with year + simple credit line

## Visual Style — Minimal Dark
- Deep near-black background (`#0B0B0E`), soft off-white text, single muted accent color (e.g. warm amber or cool slate-blue — to be picked together)
- Generous whitespace, large serif or modern sans headings, comfortable reading column (~70ch) for essays
- Subtle fade-in on scroll, no flashy animations
- Fully responsive (mobile-first, since your viewport is narrow)

## Pages

### Home
- Large name + one-line tagline ("CS student documenting the path")
- Short intro paragraph
- Three featured highlight cards (title, one-line teaser, hero image) linking to each highlight page
- Quiet link to "See all activities →"

### About me
- Portrait image, longer bio, what you study, interests
- Small list of skills/tools (optional)

### Highlight pages (×3)
- Title, date/context line, hero image
- **Essay rendered with inline images** (magazine-style): images sit between paragraphs at varying widths (full-bleed, centered, side-aligned) with optional captions
- Prev / Next highlight links at the bottom

### Activities (full list)
- Chronological list grouped by year (Year 1, Year 2, …)
- Each entry: date · title · type tag (Seminar / Hackathon / Workshop / etc.) · short one-line description · optional thumbnail
- Filter chips at top (All / Seminars / Hackathons / …)

## Content Handling
- I'll set up clear placeholder content for the 3 highlights and the activity list so you can drop in your essays and images afterward by editing simple data files — no code knowledge needed beyond pasting text and image paths
- Image uploads: you can drag images into chat and I'll wire them in

## What I'll need from you after the plan is approved
1. Your name + one-line tagline
2. The 3 highlight essays (titles + body text)
3. Any photos for the highlights, About page, and activity list (can come later)
4. Rough list of other activities for the Activities page (can be sketched and refined)

Once approved, I'll build the full site with realistic placeholder content so you can immediately see the shape, then we'll swap in your real essays and pictures.
